# Real-Time Order Processing — Customer Support Knowledge Base

This document contains official store policies, return and refund rules, and solutions to the most common customer issues. Support agents and AI assistants must answer from this document first before escalating.

---

## Return Policy Overview

Customers may return most physical products within **30 days** of delivery. This window matches industry standards used by major retailers such as Amazon, Target, and Best Buy.

The return window starts on the **delivery date**, not the order date. Digital products, gift cards, and personalized or custom-made items are **non-returnable** unless defective.

Items must be **unused**, in **original packaging**, and include all accessories, manuals, and free gifts that came with the order. Missing items may reduce the refund amount.

---

## Return Eligibility by Product Category

**Electronics and appliances** can be returned within 30 days if unopened or in like-new condition. Opened electronics may incur a **15% restocking fee** unless the item is defective or not as described.

**Clothing and footwear** must have original tags attached and show no signs of wear. Hygiene-sealed items such as underwear, swimwear, and earrings cannot be returned once opened.

**Beauty and personal care** products must be unopened and sealed. Opened cosmetics cannot be returned for hygiene reasons unless the product is defective or expired on arrival.

**Perishable goods** including food, flowers, and plants are not eligible for return unless damaged or spoiled on delivery. Report perishable issues within **24 hours** of delivery with photos.

**Final sale items** marked as clearance, warehouse deals, or "final sale" at checkout cannot be returned. Check the product page and order confirmation for final sale labels.

---

## How to Start a Return

Log in to your account and open **My Orders**. Select the order containing the item you want to return, then click **Return or Replace Items**.

Choose a return reason from the list: wrong item received, item damaged, item defective, no longer needed, or better price found elsewhere. Accurate reasons help us process returns faster.

Print the prepaid return shipping label from your account or request a pickup if available in your region. Pack the item securely in the original box when possible.

Drop off the package at the designated carrier location or wait for scheduled pickup. Keep the tracking number until the return is confirmed received at our warehouse.

---

## Return Shipping Costs

Returns due to our error — wrong item, defective product, or damaged in transit — are **free**. We provide a prepaid return label at no cost to the customer.

Returns for change of mind or "no longer needed" may incur a **return shipping fee** deducted from the refund. The fee amount is shown before you confirm the return request.

Customers with **Premium membership** receive free return shipping on all eligible items regardless of return reason. Standard members pay return shipping on change-of-mind returns only.

International returns may require the customer to pay outbound shipping. Refunds for international orders exclude original shipping charges unless the return is due to our error.

---

## Refund Policy Overview

Refunds are issued to the **original payment method** used at checkout. We do not send refunds to a different card or bank account for security reasons.

Standard refund processing takes **5 to 7 business days** after we receive and inspect the returned item. This timeline aligns with PayPal, Stripe, and major bank processing standards.

Refunds for orders paid by credit or debit card may take an additional **3 to 5 business days** to appear on your statement depending on your bank. UPI and wallet refunds typically post within **24 to 48 hours**.

If you paid using store credit or a gift card, the refund is restored to your store wallet balance. Gift card refunds cannot be converted to cash.

---

## Refund Timeline by Scenario

**Cancelled before shipment:** Full refund initiated within **24 hours**. Most payment methods reflect the credit within 3 to 5 business days.

**Returned item received at warehouse:** Inspection takes **1 to 2 business days**. Refund is triggered immediately after inspection passes.

**Partial return from multi-item order:** Each returned item is refunded separately. Shipping charges may be partially non-refundable on change-of-mind returns.

**Price adjustment requests:** If an item drops in price within **7 days** of your purchase, submit a request with your order number. We may refund the difference as store credit or to your original payment method.

**Promotional or coupon orders:** Refunds reflect the amount actually paid after discounts. Coupon value is not refunded as cash unless required by law.

---

## Refund Not Received — Troubleshooting

If your refund status shows **Completed** in your account but you do not see the money, first check with your bank or card issuer. Banks often hold pending credits before posting.

Verify the refund went to the correct payment method. Split payments are refunded proportionally to each method used at checkout.

Refunds to expired or cancelled cards are usually redirected by the bank to your active account. Contact your bank if the refund does not appear within **10 business days** after our confirmation.

If more than **10 business days** have passed since refund confirmation, a support investigation may be required. Provide your order number, payment method, and refund confirmation email. Our team can trace the transaction with the payment provider.

---

## Exchange Policy

Exchanges are available for size and color variants of the same product when stock is available. Select **Exchange** instead of **Return** when starting your request in My Orders.

If the desired variant is out of stock, we recommend returning the item for a refund and placing a new order. Exchange requests do not hold inventory until the return is initiated.

Exchanges ship at no additional cost when the exchange is due to our error. Change-of-mind exchanges follow the same return shipping rules as standard returns.

---

## Order Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled free of charge **before they enter the shipping process**. Check order status in My Orders — if status is **Processing** or **Confirmed**, cancellation is usually still possible.

Once an order status changes to **Shipped** or **Out for Delivery**, cancellation is no longer available. You may refuse delivery or start a return after delivery instead.

Pre-orders and made-to-order items can be cancelled until **48 hours before** the estimated ship date unless otherwise stated on the product page.

Cancellation refunds follow the same timeline as standard refunds. Partial cancellations of multi-item orders refund only the cancelled items.

---

## Damaged or Defective Items

Report damaged or defective items within **48 hours** of delivery. Include clear photos of the damage, packaging, and shipping label in your return or support request.

Do not discard damaged packaging until the claim is resolved. Carrier inspections sometimes require photos of the outer box and inner packaging.

Defective items receive a **full refund including shipping** or a free replacement at your choice. No restocking fee applies to defective or damaged product returns.

For high-value items over **$500**, our team may arrange a direct exchange or pickup rather than requiring you to ship the item back first.

---

## Wrong Item Received

If you received the wrong product, size, or color, report the issue within **7 days** of delivery. We will send the correct item at no charge or provide a prepaid return label and full refund.

Keep the incorrect item in original condition until we send return instructions. In most cases we do not require you to return low-value wrong items — you may keep or donate them.

Wrong item claims require your **order number** and a photo of the item received compared to what you ordered. This helps our warehouse investigate picking errors.

---

## Missing Items or Partial Delivery

If your order arrived but items are missing from the box, report it within **48 hours**. Check all packaging materials — small items are sometimes nested inside larger product boxes.

Compare the packing slip inside the box to your order confirmation. If an item is listed on the slip but not in the package, we will reship or refund the missing item.

For multi-package orders, wait until **all packages** show delivered before reporting missing items. Large orders often ship in separate boxes on different days.

If tracking shows delivered but you did not receive the package, check with neighbors, building reception, and safe drop locations first. Then report the issue within **72 hours** to open an investigation.

---

## Order Tracking Issues

Track your order from **My Orders** using the tracking link provided when the order ships. Tracking updates every **4 to 12 hours** depending on the carrier.

**Tracking not updating:** Carriers sometimes delay scans during transit. Allow **24 to 48 hours** after the shipping confirmation email before reporting stale tracking.

**Tracking shows delivered but not received:** Follow the missing package process above. We can file a carrier claim on your behalf after **72 hours** from the marked delivery time.

**Order stuck in Processing:** Most orders ship within **1 to 2 business days**. During peak sale periods, processing may take up to **5 business days**. You will receive an email when the order ships.

**Escalation trigger — order stuck in Processing:** If the customer reports that:
- the order has not shipped after **3 business days**, OR
- the customer requests an investigation, OR
- tracking information appears incorrect,

the support assistant must **immediately create a support ticket** using the logged-in customer's user ID and email from context. Include a brief issue description from the conversation. Do not ask for phone or email again.

**International orders:** Customs clearance can add **3 to 10 business days**. Tracking may pause at customs — this is normal and not a lost package.

---

## Shipping and Delivery Delays

Standard domestic delivery takes **3 to 7 business days** after shipment. Express delivery takes **1 to 2 business days** in eligible regions.

Weather, holidays, and carrier volume can cause delays beyond estimated delivery dates. Check the carrier tracking page for the most current estimated delivery window.

If your order is **more than 3 business days** past the estimated delivery date and tracking has not updated, a support investigation may be required.

For delayed pre-orders, we send email updates when the ship date changes. You may cancel pre-orders without penalty if the new ship date is unacceptable.

---

## Payment Issues

**Payment declined at checkout:** Verify card details, billing address matches bank records, and sufficient funds are available. Try an alternate payment method or contact your bank.

**Double charge:** Pending authorizations often appear as duplicate charges but only one settles. Wait **3 to 5 business days** for pending charges to drop. If both charges post, this issue requires further investigation.

**Razorpay or UPI payment failed but money deducted:** Failed UPI transactions usually reverse within **24 to 48 hours** automatically. If not reversed within 48 hours, provide the UPI transaction ID for further investigation.

**Refund to wrong account:** Refunds always go to the original payment method. We cannot redirect refunds. If your card was replaced, contact your bank to trace the refund to your new card.

**Invoice or GST receipt:** Download invoices from **My Orders → Order Details → Download Invoice**. For GST corrections, request a correction within **30 days** of purchase with your GSTIN.

---

## Account and Login Issues

**Forgot password:** Use **Forgot Password** on the login page. A reset link is sent to your registered email within **5 minutes**. Check spam folder if not received.

**Account locked after failed logins:** Wait **30 minutes** or use password reset to unlock immediately. Accounts lock after **5 consecutive** failed login attempts for security.

**Email change requests:** For security, email changes require verification from both old and new email addresses. Further verification is required if you no longer have access to your old email.

**Delete account:** Account deletion is available in **Account Settings → Privacy → Delete Account**. Active orders must be completed or cancelled before deletion. Order history is retained for legal and tax requirements.

---

## Promo Codes and Discounts

Promo codes must be entered at checkout before payment. Codes cannot be applied to completed orders retroactively except at support discretion within **24 hours**.

Each promo code has specific terms: minimum order value, eligible categories, expiration date, and single-use limits. Read the promotion terms on the offer page.

**Code not working:** Verify the code is spelled correctly, not expired, and your cart meets minimum requirements. Some codes exclude sale items or specific brands.

One promo code per order unless stated otherwise. Store credit and promo codes can usually be combined — gift cards may have restrictions noted at checkout.

---

## Membership and Subscriptions

**Premium membership** includes free shipping, free returns, early access to sales, and priority support. Membership renews automatically unless cancelled before the renewal date.

Cancel membership anytime in **Account Settings → Membership → Cancel**. You retain benefits until the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds for unused months.

Subscription products such as monthly boxes renew on a fixed schedule. Skip or cancel upcoming shipments at least **3 days before** the next billing date to avoid charges.

---

## Warranty and Manufacturer Support

Products include a **manufacturer warranty** where applicable. Warranty length and terms vary by product — see the product page or included warranty card.

Our store warranty covers **defects in materials and workmanship** for **1 year** from delivery on eligible electronics unless a longer manufacturer warranty applies.

Warranty claims require proof of purchase from our store. Provide your order number, product serial number, and description of the defect. We coordinate with manufacturers when needed.

Warranty does not cover accidental damage, water damage, unauthorized repairs, or normal wear and tear. Extended protection plans may be purchased at checkout for additional coverage.

---

## Escalation

**Self-service first:** Most issues can be resolved using My Orders for returns, tracking, cancellations, and invoice downloads without agent assistance.

**Live chat support** hours are **Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 8 PM** local time. Response time for follow-up is within **24 hours** on business days.

When escalating an issue, always provide your **order number**, **registered email**, and a **brief description** of the issue. Photos help for damage, wrong item, and missing item claims.

**Escalation criteria:** Escalate when self-service options do not apply, refund is overdue beyond 10 business days, high-value order issues exceed **$500**, or legal and tax documentation is required.

After creating a support ticket for unresolved issues, confirm that the support team will follow up within **24 to 48 business hours**. Do not keep the customer waiting in chat once the ticket is created.

## Common Issue Quick Solutions

### I want to return my order
Go to My Orders → select order → Return or Replace Items. Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery for eligible items in original condition.

### Where is my refund
Refunds process within 5 to 7 business days after we receive your return. Check My Orders for refund status. Allow extra 3 to 5 days for bank processing.

### My order has not arrived
Check tracking in My Orders. Allow 24 hours for tracking updates after shipping. If tracking shows delivered but you did not receive it, report the issue within 72 hours to open an investigation.

### I was charged twice
One charge may be a pending authorization that drops in 3 to 5 days. If both charges post, this issue requires further investigation — provide your order number and bank statement screenshot.

### I received a damaged item
Report within 48 hours with photos. Choose Return or Replace in My Orders and select "Item damaged." Full refund or free replacement includes return shipping.

### I got the wrong item
Report within 7 days with photos and order number. We send a prepaid return label or let you keep low-value items while we ship the correct product.

### I want to cancel my order
Cancel in My Orders if status is Processing or Confirmed. Once Shipped, refuse delivery or return after delivery. Refund posts within 24 hours of cancellation.

### Promo code did not work
Verify code spelling, expiration, and minimum order value. Some codes exclude sale items. Codes must be applied at checkout and cannot be added after payment.

### I cannot log in
Use Forgot Password on the login page. Check spam for reset email. Account unlocks after 30 minutes or immediately via password reset.

### How do I track my order
Open My Orders and click the tracking link on shipped orders. Tracking updates every 4 to 12 hours. If tracking is unchanged for 48 hours after shipping, a support investigation may be required.

### Item missing from my package
Report within 48 hours. Check all packaging and packing slip. Multi-package orders may arrive separately — wait for all packages to show delivered.

### I need an invoice
Download from My Orders → Order Details → Download Invoice. For GST invoice corrections, request a correction within 30 days with your GSTIN.

### Payment failed but money was deducted
UPI and wallet failures usually reverse within 24 to 48 hours. Provide the transaction ID for further investigation if not reversed after 48 hours.

### I want to exchange for different size
Use Exchange option in My Orders if stock is available. If out of stock, return for refund and place a new order.

### My return was rejected
Returns are rejected if items are used, missing packaging, or outside the 30-day window. Check the rejection reason in My Orders and request an appeal with evidence if you disagree.

### Membership cancellation
Cancel in Account Settings → Membership → Cancel before renewal date. Benefits continue until end of current billing period.
